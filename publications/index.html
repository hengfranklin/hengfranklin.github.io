publications

Selected publications and patents in reverse chronological order.

2025

  1. Cureus
    Medication Beliefs Regarding P2Y12 Inhibitors: An Exploratory Thematic and Semantic Analysis to Better Understand the Key Barriers to Medication Adherence
    Jae Lee, Franklin Heng, and M. Kesarwani
    Cureus, 2025
    Abs

    Thematic and semantic analysis of patient-reported barriers to P2Y12 inhibitor adherence, using NLP to surface medication-belief themes from open-ended survey responses.

2023

  1. JCI Insight
    Mucus Plugs in Proximal Airway Generations Are Consequential for Airflow Limitation in Asthma
    Bo Huang, others, Franklin Heng, and 2 more authors
    JCI Insight, 2023
    Abs HTML

    A novel CT-derived airway-resistance score, built from automated mucus-plug segmentation and per-airway-generation mapping, correlates with FEV1 decline across 580+ asthma patients.

2022

  1. US Patent
    Methods for Pulmonary Function Testing With Machine Learning Analysis and Systems for Same
    Franklin Heng and Xavier Orain
    2022
    US Patent Application US20240090795A1, filed February 2022
    Abs HTML

    Method and system for contactless pulmonary function testing from depth-camera video of chest wall movement, designed for patients who cannot form a seal around a traditional spirometer mouthpiece.

  2. NAPPN
    Computer Vision Based Phenotyping Approaches in the Brown Macroalgae, Saccharina Latissima
    Franklin Heng, J. Belanger, and others
    In NAPPN Annual Conference, 2022
    Abs HTML

    2D and 3D imaging pipelines for phenotyping farmed kelp, combining classical CV, Faster R-CNN detection, and Structure-from-Motion reconstruction with ArUco-based metric scaling.

2019

  1. Electronic Imaging
    In Situ Width Estimation of Biofuel Plant Stems
    A. Sahiner, Franklin Heng, A. Balamurugan, and 1 more author
    In Electronic Imaging Conference, 2019
    Abs HTML

    In-field stem-width estimation for biofuel sorghum from a moving robotic platform, fusing Faster R-CNN detection with Wiener-filtered Canny edges and RANSAC boundary fitting against paired depth.
